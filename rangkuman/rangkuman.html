Unity and Coherence

Definisi

1. Unity

Unity means "oneness." A paragraph must deal with only one idea.

2. Coherence

Coherence means "a sticking together." A coherent paragraph has all the sentences so well arranged that not one could be interchanged with another. It occurs when one idea connects clearly and smoothly to the next.

Contoh Paragraf

Paragraf Tanpa Unity dan Coherence:

"I am happy that the warm weather is finally here! It's been a cold winter. There are several reasons to select my company to do this job. By far, we are the best company to complete this project. I have a large family, and in addition to having Sunday dinners, we work together in the company, which has many satisfied customers. Some of my employees take the bus to work, so I am concerned about our public transportation system. We have proudly served our community, and we use cost-saving methods to keep prices low."

Paragraf dengan Unity dan Coherence:

"There are several reasons to select my company to do this job. We are a family-owned and operated business and have been in business in this county for thirty-five years. In addition to thousands of satisfied customers, we have proudly sponsored many community events and organizations. All of our employees live in this county, and most have stayed with us for years. We have successfully kept our overhead low and pass those savings onto our customers. By far, we are the best company to complete this project."

Catatan: Semua kalimat bekerja sama untuk mendukung poin bahwa kami adalah perusahaan terbaik untuk disewa.

Transitions: Kata-kata yang Menghubungkan (Kohesi)

Transitions adalah kata, kelompok kata, atau kalimat yang menghubungkan satu kalimat ke kalimat lain atau satu paragraf ke paragraf lain. Menambahkan transisi disebut kohesi. Menciptakan kohesi berarti mengikat kata, frasa, kalimat, dan paragraf kita bersama untuk menciptakan hubungan dan kelancaran dalam teks.

Berbagai Jenis Transisi:

  • Konjungsi: Kata-kata yang menghubungkan dua atau lebih ide (misalnya, dan, juga, selain itu).
  • Contoh: Misalnya, satu contoh adalah...
  • Perbandingan: Di sisi lain, pandangan yang berlawanan...
  • Akibat: Akibatnya, selanjutnya...
  • Hubungan Sebab-Akibat: Oleh karena itu, karena, akibatnya...
  • Kesamaan: Demikian pula, dengan cara yang sama, dengan cara yang serupa...
  • Perbedaan: Namun, sebaliknya, di sisi lain...
  • Hubungan Waktu: Sebelum, setelah, sementara, kemudian, setelah, selama, dalam waktu yang sama, saat ini.

Transisi ini membantu untuk melukiskan hubungan yang jelas dan logis antara kalimat dalam sebuah paragraf.
